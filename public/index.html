Handshake sequence

  1. Open CROSSWIRE in two tabs or on two devices.
  2. Tab A: hit Create offer, copy Station A's box.
  3. Tab B: paste it into Station B, hit Connect.
  4. Tab B: copy the reply from its Station A box.
  5. Tab A: paste the reply into Station B, hit Connect.
  6. Link goes live — drop a file below. Max 50 MB.
